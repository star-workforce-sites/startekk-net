Product Overview

Star Automation goes beyond traditional RPA (Robotic Process Automation). While competitors require extensive programming and break when workflows change, our AI-powered platform learns your processes by watching employees work, adapts to changes automatically, and handles exceptions intelligently without constant maintenance.

From data entry and invoice processing to customer onboarding and report generation—if a human does it repeatedly on a computer, Star Automation can learn it and do it better.
