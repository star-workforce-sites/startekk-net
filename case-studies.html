INSURANCE • SAN JOSE, CA
Email Automation & Cloud Migration

The Challenge

A mid-sized insurance company in San Jose was drowning in customer emails—processing 500+ daily inquiries manually with 1-2 day response times. Their aging on-premise infrastructure limited scalability and their small team couldn't keep up with growth.

Our Solution

We deployed an offshore team of 32 resources combining RPA bots with NLP for intelligent email routing and auto-responses. Simultaneously, we migrated their infrastructure to AWS with auto-scaling capabilities and implemented 24/7 monitoring.

The Results

  • 4-hour response time (down from 1-2 days)
  • 20% cost savings annually
  • 80% email categorization accuracy
  • 99.9% uptime with AWS infrastructure

"StarTekk's offshore team and automation expertise transformed our operations. What used to take us days now happens in hours, and our customer satisfaction scores have never been higher. The AWS migration was seamless, and the cost savings are substantial."

— VP of Operations, Insurance Company
