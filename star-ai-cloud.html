Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from AWS Cost Explorer or Azure Cost Management?

AWS Cost Explorer shows you WHAT you spent. Star AI Cloud tells you HOW to save. We provide specific, actionable recommendations with dollar amounts, Terraform code, and one-click apply. Native tools require manual analysis.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use read-only OAuth access (we can't modify your resources). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We're SOC 2 Type II certified. We never share your data with third parties.

Will this work for multi-cloud environments?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans support AWS, Azure, and GCP simultaneously. We analyze each cloud separately and identify cross-cloud opportunities (e.g., moving workloads to cheaper clouds).

How long does setup take?

2 minutes. You connect your cloud account via OAuth, and we start scanning immediately. First recommendations appear within 24 hours after the initial scan completes.

Do you automatically make changes to my infrastructure?

NO. We NEVER make changes without your explicit approval. You review every recommendation, then choose to apply it manually or use our one-click apply (with approval workflow). You're always in control.

What about the ROI guarantee?

We guarantee to find at least 10x your subscription cost in savings within 90 days, or we refund 100% of your money. For example, if you're on the $199/mo plan, we guarantee to find $1,990/mo in savings opportunities.

Do you offer custom plans for large enterprises?

Yes! Enterprise customers with complex needs can get custom pricing, dedicated support, custom integrations, and volume discounts. Contact our sales team for a tailored quote.

Can Star AI Cloud integrate with our CI/CD pipeline?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include API access. You can integrate our recommendations into your GitOps workflow, auto-create PRs for savings opportunities, and include cost optimization in your deployment pipeline.
