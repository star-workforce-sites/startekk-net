Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience and understand how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your login state and form data
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Cookie Consent: Remember your cookie preferences

2. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

  • Google Analytics: Track page views, session duration, bounce rate
  • Performance Monitoring: Measure page load times and errors

3. Functional Cookies (Optional)

Enhance functionality and personalization.

  • Preferences: Remember language, region, display settings
  • Chat Widget: If you use our support chat

Managing Cookies

You have several options to control cookies:

Cookie Banner

Use our cookie consent banner to accept, decline, or customize which cookies you allow.

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when closing browser

Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

GDPR Compliance

Under GDPR, we:

  • Obtain consent before setting non-essential cookies
  • Allow you to withdraw consent at any time
  • Provide clear information about cookie purposes
  • Use anonymized IP addresses in Google Analytics

Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy
  • Vercel (hosting): Privacy Policy
