Common Employment Scams

Scammers may attempt to:

  • Request payment for job applications, background checks, or training
  • Ask for sensitive personal information before an official offer
  • Conduct interviews via instant messaging only (no video or phone)
  • Send checks and ask you to wire money back
  • Offer unrealistic salaries for minimal work
  • Use unofficial email addresses (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.)
  • Pressure you to act immediately
  • Request bank account or credit card information
