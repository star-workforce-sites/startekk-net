What StarLegalAI Surfaces

Six layers of AI-powered contract intelligence — everything that matters, nothing that doesn't.

Contract Summary

Plain-language overview of every key agreement point — what was agreed, by whom, and under what conditions — without reading 40 pages yourself.

Risk Detection

Flags unfavorable clauses, uncapped liability, unusual indemnification terms, and missing protections before they become six-figure problems.

Key Obligations

Maps who owes what, by when — payment terms, performance requirements, and party-specific duties laid out clearly in one view.

Deadline Extraction

Surfaces renewal dates, notice periods, option exercise windows, and performance deadlines — nothing buried on page 34 slips through.

AI Follow-Up Q&A

Ask any question about the contract in plain English and get an instant, accurate answer — no re-reading, no searching through pages.

Plain English Mode

Translates dense legalese into client-ready language for faster sign-off — share directly with your client without re-drafting a summary yourself.
