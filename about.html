Our Story

Startekk, LLC (dba STAR Workforce Solutions) is a leading provider of AI-powered business solutions with over 20 years of combined experience in technology innovation and business transformation.

We specialize in delivering cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions that help businesses automate operations, reduce costs, and accelerate growth. Our mission is to make advanced AI technology accessible and practical for organizations of all sizes.

Based in Plano, Texas, we serve clients across the United States, Canada, and internationally, delivering measurable results with an average 30% cost savings and 3-5% ROI improvement.
